Commissions

Commissions for songs are currently CLOSED.

Commissioned material can be used in personal, non-profit, or commercial endeavors. The individual requesting the commission may choose how they wish to distribute use permissions to others. If the commission is used publically, remember to credit me.

Please be aware of my genre, because I have more experiences in certain styles than in others, and the time a commission takes will reflect this.

Pricing is determined by the length of the piece, as listed below. The price of a loop is based on the length of one cycle of the loop.

0–2 Minutes  $2

2–4 Minutes  $3

4–8 Minutes  $4

I accept payment via PayPal. If you want to arrange a commission, contact me via Discord or Twitter.
